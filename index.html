The Studio

Patina Design Studio is a boutique interior architecture practice based in Sydney, dedicated to the art of bespoke residential design.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every space should tell a story. We blend textures, light, and form to create interiors that are not only beautiful but deeply personal and functional.
